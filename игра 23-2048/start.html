2048 is an easy-to-play number puzzle game. This simple but addictive number game was released in 2014, created by Gabriele Cirulli, and based on the 1024 puzzle game. Since its release, it has become a viral hit.

HOW TO PLAY: Use your arrow keys (, , , ) to move the tiles. Every time you press an arrow key, all tiles will slide. When two tiles of the same number touch, they merge into one. Add them up to reach the final 2048 tile and win the game!
